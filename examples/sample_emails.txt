# Sample Phishing Emails for Testing

## Phishing Email 1 - Account Suspension
Subject: URGENT: Your Account Will Be Suspended
From: security@amaz0n-security.com

Dear Valued Customer,

We have detected unusual activity on your account. Your account will be suspended within 24 hours unless you verify your information immediately.

Click here to verify: http://amaz0n-verify.suspicious-site.com/login

This is an automated message. Do not reply to this email.

Best regards,
Amazon Security Team

## Phishing Email 2 - Prize Winner
Subject: Congratulations! You've Won $50,000
From: winnings@prize-central.org

Dear Lucky Winner,

Congratulations! You have been selected as the winner of our annual cash prize of $50,000.

To claim your prize, please provide the following information:
- Full Name
- Address
- Phone Number
- Social Security Number
- Bank Account Details

Reply to this email with your information within 48 hours or your prize will be forfeited.

Thank you,
Prize Distribution Center

## Phishing Email 3 - Banking Alert
Subject: Security Alert: Suspicious Login Detected
From: alerts@secure-banking-center.com

Important Security Notice

We have detected a suspicious login attempt to your account from an unrecognized device.

Account Details:
- Login Time: 2024-01-15 14:32:15
- Location: Unknown
- Device: Unrecognized

Please verify this activity by clicking the link below:
https://secure-bank-verification.malicious-site.com

If you did not authorize this login, please contact us immediately.

Security Team
Online Banking Services

## Legitimate Email 1 - Order Confirmation
Subject: Your Order Confirmation #12345
From: orders@legitimate-store.com

Dear Customer,

Thank you for your recent purchase. Your order #12345 has been confirmed and will be shipped within 2-3 business days.

Order Details:
- Item: Wireless Headphones
- Quantity: 1
- Total: $99.99
- Shipping Address: [Your Address]

You can track your order at: https://legitimate-store.com/track/12345

Thank you for shopping with us!

Customer Service Team
Legitimate Store

## Legitimate Email 2 - Password Reset Confirmation
Subject: Password Reset Successful
From: security@real-company.com

Hello,

Your password has been successfully reset for your account associated with this email address.

If you did not request this password reset, please contact our support team immediately at support@real-company.com or call 1-800-SUPPORT.

For your security:
- Never share your password with anyone
- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication

Best regards,
Security Team
Real Company Inc.
